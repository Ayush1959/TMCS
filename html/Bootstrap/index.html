Founded in 1981, Venad Pravasi' Society is one of the oldest and largest pravasi society in Pathanamthitta district. VPS strives to preserve and promote the Malayalee's social, cultural , educational, literary and artistic heritage through it's programs and initiatives.

We welcome all Malayalee families who are present in the Pathanamthitta area to join us!
